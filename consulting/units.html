Imperial Units¶

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Imperial_units
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/English_units

Imperial Units may refer to either English Units (ended in 1824) or US Customary Units (e.g. gram, ounce, gallon, pound, foot, mile).

  • The UK (United Kingdom) (of which England is a part) now specifies Metric Units.
  • The USA (United States of America) US Customary Units still include many Imperial Units.

Imperial Distance Units¶

Inch¶

  • Conversion: 1 Inch == 2.54 centimeters

Foot¶

  • Conversion: 1 Foot == 12 inches

Mile¶

  • Conversion: 1 Mile == 5280 feet
  • Conversion: 1 Mile == 63360 inches

Imperial Fluid Units¶

Fluid Ounce¶

  • Fluid Ounce (Fluid Ounce)
  • Abbrev: fl/oz
  • Abbrev: oz
  • Serving size: 1 US fluid serving == 8 oz
  • Serving size: 1 can of e.g. soda == 12oz (== 1.5 US fluid serving)

Pint¶

  • Conversion: 1 Pint == 16 oz
  • Example: 1 plastic bottle of soda

Gallon¶

  • Conversion: 1 Gallon == 128 oz
  • Conversion: 1 Gallon == 8 pints

Keg¶

  • Conversion: 1 Keg == 124 US pints
  • Conversion: 1 Keg == 1984 oz
